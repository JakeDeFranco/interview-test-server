Description:

Welcome! Please build one of the following applications in either: React.js or Python.

There are two levels for each assignment, level 1 being the easiest, and level 2 containing some 'gotchas' and other unexpected behaviour. If you complete the first level with ease, feel free to jump to the next level, but please timebox your solution to a maximum of one day.

Assignment Options:

Tax Calculator

Calculate the total amount owed in taxes based on an income.

Autonomous Car

Navigate a car based on a provided track and instructions.
